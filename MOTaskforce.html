Mental Omega 3.3.4 Taskforces Generator (v1.0)

Created by
[ISS]whenson


Left click unit icons to add it into the currently selected taskforce.
Right click unit icons to remove it from the taskforce.
Ctrl/Shift click to add/remove 5 units at a time.
Click the "Generate!" button to generate taskforces in copyable text.
Open your map file with notepad and paste the text into it.

: Show All Units

/

: Clear all units from current taskforce
